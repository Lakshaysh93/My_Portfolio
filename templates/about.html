03 : About me

Biography

I was born in Patiala, a tiny town in India. Since I was young, computers have piqued my curiosity.This increased my motivation to seek a bachelor's degree in technology.I vividly recall how I used to play around with apps on my friend's computer and the computer at the college for hours.

Education

In 2015, I graduated with a bachelor's degree in technology from the Rayat Bahra Institute of Technology's Patiala, Behdpura Campus.I moved to Canada in 2021 to follow my ambition of attending graduate school after working for a few years in a number of IT organizations.In December 2021, I received my Masters of Engineering in Software Systems Engineering from the Université of Regina.

Interests

I've always had a strong interest in experimenting with new technology.From a young age, I found amusement in computers, and that has always been my field of interest.In addition, I find astronomy and travel to be quite entertaining.The time I spent travelling alone to Begrade, Serbia, was my favourite.And I enjoy learning new space-related facts.In the future, I want to create content about space science and upload it to my website.
